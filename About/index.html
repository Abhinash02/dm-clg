About D.M. COLLEGE, MOGA

       Dayanand Mathra Dass College, Moga was started in 1962 by Rai Bahadur Dr. Mathra Dass Pahwa, the padam Vibhushan awardee and the eminent eye surgeon, philanthropist and educationist in the sacred memory of Mharishi Dayanand Saraswati. It is one of the premier Co-Educational institutions of the state which has been serving the society for many decades.
      The college since its inception has introduced many faculties like Science. Commerce, Humanities, Languages and Computer Science.The college also introduced Post Graduated Courses in English, Punjabi and Hindi to permote job oriented certificate courses in the subject fashion Designing, Computer Based Accounting.

AFFILIATION

       The College is affiliated to the Panjab University Chandigarh for undergraduate courses like B.A/B.Com/ B.Sc./B.C.A., Post Graduate Courses like PGDCA, M.A. (Eng., Pbi., Hindi), M.Sc. Mathematics and all Add-On-Courses like Computer Based Accounting, Insurance Business, Fashion Designing.
