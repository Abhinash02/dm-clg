THE Awardee of "PADAM VIBHUSAN" and the eminent eye surgeon, philanthropist and educationist in the Sacred memory of the Maharishi Swami Dayanand Saraswati. It is one of the eminent and excellent institutions of the State. It has been the envy and emulation of the neighboring institutions for many a decade. It has been holding aloft the banner of light and learning for the last eighty two years and has been serving Moga and its surrounding areas of Punjab, Haryana and Himachal with a singleness of purpose and devotion in the sphere of higher education. The institution has been invested with a lofty mission of dispelling the darkness and ignorance. It aims at the harmonious development of the students in the physical, mental and spiritual spheres. It has proved to be the singular institution in many a respect throughout the length and breadth of the country. The institution, since its inception, has introduced many faculties like M.A.(Economics), B.A.(Honors), B.Sc.(Medical),B.com, Post Graduate Diploma course in Personnel Management and Industrial Relations. Recently the new courses of B.CA. P.G.D.C.A and office Management, Music, Physical Education and Computer Science have been introduced.
